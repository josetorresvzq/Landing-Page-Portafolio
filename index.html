How we work on your project

A simple three-step process so you know exactly what to expect from first contact to final delivery.

01

Understanding your idea

We briefly review your context, goals, and references. We define what pieces you need and what you want the design to achieve.

02

Visual proposal

I present a clear visual direction (identity, layout, styles) and adjust it with you until it fully represents your project.

03

Ready-to-use delivery

I deliver final organized files ready for use in presentations, social media, print or other formats you need.
